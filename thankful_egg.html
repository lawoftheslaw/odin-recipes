Thankful Eggs

About the Eggs

You'll be thankful you tried them :)

Ingredients

  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie filling
  • 1/4 piece graham cracker
  • 2 toasted marshmallows
  • 1 teaspoon caramel sauce

Steps

  1. Scoop out the yolk in a hard-boiled egg.
  2. Replace it with pumpkin pie filling.
  3. Top it with a graham cracker, toasted marshmallows, and caramel sauce.
  4. You'll be thankful you tried them :)
